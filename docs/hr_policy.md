# ACME Corp HR Leave Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2025  
Owner: ACME Corp HR Operations Team  
Department: Human Resources  
Status: Active  

## Section 1: Annual Leave Allowance
ACME Corp full-time employees accrue paid annual leave at a rate of 2.08 days per month, totaling 25 vacation days per calendar year. Paid annual leave must be requested at least 14 days in advance and requires manager approval. Up to 5 unused vacation days can be rolled over to the next calendar year, and these rolled-over days will expire on March 31 of that next year.

## Section 2: Sick and Personal Leave
All ACME Corp employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave annually for personal illness or family care. Sick leave does not roll over and cannot be cashed out upon separation. A doctor's note is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days. Personal leave (up to 3 days out of the 10 sick leave allowance) can be used for urgent personal business.

## Section 3: Parental Leave Policy
Eligible ACME Corp employees receive up to 12 weeks of fully paid parental leave following the birth, adoption, or foster placement of a child. Employees must have completed at least 12 consecutive months of employment to qualify. Written notice of intent to take parental leave must be submitted at least 30 days prior to the expected leave date. Paid parental leave runs concurrently with FMLA benefits.
