## Section 1: Welcome and Onboarding
Welcome to ACME Corp! Your first week will consist of onboarding activities designed to get you setup.
1. Day 1: HR verification, ACME email setup, and security access provision.
2. Day 2: Welcome session with your manager, review team goals, and set up your local development workspace.
3. Day 3-5: Complete mandatory compliance modules (including security awareness training and data privacy training).
Mandatory compliance training must be completed within 14 days of your official start date.

## Section 2: Code of Conduct
ACME Corp maintains a professional, collaborative environment. All employees are expected to behave respectfully and professionally. Harassment, discrimination, or abusive conduct of any kind is strictly prohibited and constitutes grounds for immediate termination. We enforce a flexible remote work model; however, core operational hours are designated as 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM EST, during which all team members must be reachable.

## Section 3: Equipment and Home Office Setup
ACME Corp provides standard equipment to all full-time employees, including a company-issued laptop, mouse, and power accessories. These items remain company property and must be returned upon separation. Eligible remote workers can request a home office stipend of up to $500 to cover ergonomic chairs or external monitors. Receipts must be uploaded to the Expensify portal within 30 days of purchase.
